What is monday Smart Call?

monday Smart Call is a native calling feature inside the monday CRM mobile app. Each rep receives a new, dedicated US virtual business number — a VoIP number that works over the internet (similar to WhatsApp or FaceTime Audio).

Using this dedicated number is what allows us to record, transcribe, and summarize every call automatically — something that isn't possible with regular phone calls. The prospect sees the business number as caller ID — your reps' personal numbers are never exposed.

No manual logging. No scribbled notes. No forgotten follow-ups.

You tap a contact, have the conversation, and hang up. monday handles the rest: the call is recorded, transcribed, and summarized by AI — and it all appears on the contact's timeline, ready for you and your manager to review.

About inbound calls

During the alpha, inbound calls to your monday number will hear an automated message. This does not block your personal phone — prospects can still reach you at your regular number as always.

Caller ID Option

Prefer to show your personal number?

If your reps want their personal number to appear on outbound calls (so prospects recognize who's calling and can call back directly), we can verify their existing number.

How it works: Outbound calls display the rep's real number as caller ID. Callbacks go to the rep's personal phone as usual.

Tradeoff: Callbacks to the personal number are not recorded or auto-logged by monday.

This is optional. The default uses the dedicated monday business number.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my clients see a new phone number?
By default, yes — each rep gets a dedicated US business number. If you'd prefer to show the rep's personal number on outbound calls, we can set up Caller ID verification (see the Caller ID section above).
What about inbound calls?
During the alpha, inbound calls to the monday number play an automated message. Your personal phone is unaffected — prospects can still call your regular number. Full inbound calling is on the roadmap.
Does it cost anything during the alpha?
No. Numbers and minutes are completely free during the alpha period. Post-alpha pricing will be determined, and you'll know well in advance.
Are calls recorded?
Yes — calls are recorded so we can transcribe and summarize them. A consent notification plays at the start of each call. If you have legal or compliance requirements, let us know and we'll work through them together.
How much time does this take from my team?
Minimal. Reps spend 90 seconds on a weekly survey. The champion does a quick check-in every 1–2 weeks. Smart Call is designed to save time, not cost it.
What happens after the alpha ends?
If the feature works well, it moves to a broader beta and eventually becomes a generally available monday CRM feature. Your team transitions to the production version with more capabilities.
Do I need to install anything?
Your reps need the monday mobile app on iOS. We'll walk you through number activation during a quick setup session — under 30 minutes.
